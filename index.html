Waters and Land Use of the Middle Ohio–Laughery Watershed

This interactive map shows the hydrology of the Middle Ohio–Laughery Watershed (HU8 05090203), including major rivers, streams, and waterbodies. It also highlights land use features such as wetlands and national parks. Labels for flowlines and waterbodies appear when zoomed in. State and county layers provide geographic context, along with a satellite basemap to enhance spatial understanding.

Map Details

Created by Anna Young in Mapbox Studio, with Hydrological Unit (HU) 8 - 05090203 (published 20231216) Shapefile from the USGS National Hydrography Dataset (NHD). Data was added to QGIS 3.44.2, where it was simplified and exported as GeoJSON before uploading to Mapbox Studio for interactive web mapping. Layers include rivers, streams, waterbodies, wetlands, and national parks. © USGS © Mapbox © OpenStreetMap
